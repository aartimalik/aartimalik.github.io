I am a first-year Economics PhD student at MIT, with interests in development economics.

Before my PhD, I worked with [Henrik Kleven](https://www.henrikkleven.com/) at Princeton on gender inequality in the labor market, and with [Delphine Samuels](https://www.insead.edu/faculty/delphine-samuels) at the University of Chicago Booth School of Business on questions related to financial disclosures. I also worked with [Kristina Hallez](https://cega.berkeley.edu/user-type/staff/#kristina-hallez), [Supreet Kaur](https://www.supreetkaur.com/), [Ye Rang Park](https://lcdlab.berkeley.edu/people/), and the team at [CEGA](https://cega.berkeley.edu/) on an RCT studying the effect of a direct cash transfer on parent-child interactions.

I am a tennis fan and love playing badminton (and all racket sports). I also enjoy watching movies. If you'd like to chat about your (or mine) research interests, data, tennis, or movies, please [get in touch](mailto:aartim@mit.edu). I'd love to hear from you!
